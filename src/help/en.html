poliqarp4djview

Janusz S. Bień

June 9, 2012

This is the graphical remote client for Poliqarp for DjVu corpus tool servers. It is a fork of djview4 (http://djvu.sourceforge.net/djview4.html).

The corpus queries are documented elsewhere, in particular in http://korpus.pl/en/cheatsheet/ and http://poliqarp.sourceforge.net/man/poliqarp-query-syntax.html; examples of use can be find e.g. in several papers and presentations available in Digital Library of the Formal Linguistics Department at the University of Warsaw (http://bc.klf.uw.edu.pl/).

Before the first use of the client it is necessary to add at least one address of a server. It is done from Settings --- Configure --- Servers panel.

On MS Windows it may be necessary to set Settings --- Configure --- Path to djview4.

The use of the client is self-explanatory with some minor exceptions (some of them are inherited from djview4):

  • a single left click in the side panel text area selects the appropriate hit and synchronizes the graphical, textual and metadata panel,
  • a single left click in the side panel on a hit number selects it and displays its metadata,
  • a double left click in the side panel text area displays the appropriate page in the main window,
  • a middle click in the side panel text area starts a new instance of djview4 with the appropriate page displayed,
  • pressing the left button allows to pan the text in the window,
  • pressing Ctrl key allows to use the mouse wheel for zooming the current window.

The recommended way to report bugs and problems is to send them to the mailing list: http://lists.mimuw.edu.pl/listinfo/nmpt-l.
